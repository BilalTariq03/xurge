Introduction

At Xurge Studio, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or reach out to work with us.

We believe in transparency — no unnecessary data collection, no hidden practices. Just clear, honest communication about how your information is handled.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions at any point, don't hesitate to reach out.

02

Information We Collect

We collect two types of information — what you give us directly, and what's collected automatically when you browse our site.

Personal Information — when you contact us or enquire about our services:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name or project details
  • Billing information (only when required for invoicing)

Usage Data — collected automatically when you visit our website:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each
  • Referring website or search query
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to run our business honestly and serve you well. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide the services you've requested
  • Send project updates, proposals, and relevant communications
  • Process payments and manage billing
  • Improve our website's design, performance, and content
  • Analyse usage patterns to better understand our audience
  • Meet legal or regulatory obligations where required

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

05

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience and help us analyse how the site is used.

  • Essential Cookies — Required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand visitor behaviour and improve our content.
  • Preference Cookies — Remember your settings for a better experience.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect how parts of the website function.
